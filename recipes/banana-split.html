The Classic Banana Split

Nothing shouts American Summer quite like a Classic Banana Split

A banana split is one of those saacred treats only offered on birthdays or bereavements. A strange mix of love and shame.

In this receipe I will share a classic version of a banana split. Although please do no think that you are limited to following this receipe exactly. Part of the joy of a banana split is allowing yourself the fun to just play.

So...Without further ado...

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Scoop Vanilla Icecream
  • 1 Scoop Chocolate Icecream
  • 1 Scoop Strawberry Icecream
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Marshmallow Creme
  • Whipped Cream
  • Chopped, Lightly Toasted Nuts
  • 3 Maraschino Cherries

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Peel the banana and slice it in half lengthwise. Place the banana slices against the sides of a long, narrow, shallow dish.
  3. Place the scoops of ice cream in a row between the banana slices.
  4. Squirt the chocolate syrup on the vanilla and strawberry ice cream.
  5. Spoon the marshmallow creme over the chocolate ice cream.
  6. Top everything with the whipped cream. Sprinkle it all with nuts and top each mound of ice cream with a cherry.
