A Quick 30 Minute Chicken Pho

This Quick Chicken Pho is both Relaxing to make and Soothing for the Soul.

Where do you turn when you arent feeling well, and nothing seems to be hitting the spot? A Chicken Pho. What is pho? (Pronounced 'foe') Chicken Pho is a soup dish originating from Vietnam. It is a rich broth with simple fresh vegetables and chicken added, topped with a soft rice flat noodle.

If you have never had a pho, or experienced the transformative properties that it imbues, then hold on to your seats, because this simple recipe is going to blow your mind!

Ingredients

For 4 Servings
  • 10 oz rice noodle (285 g), dried, narrow, and flat
  • warm water, for soaking
  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorn
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seed
  • 4 pods whole star anise
  • 1 piece fresh ginger, 2 inch (5 cm) piece
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 4 cups hot water (960 mL)
  • 8 cups chicken broth (2 L)
  • 1 lb chicken breast (455 g), halved, crosswise
  • ¼ cup fish sauce (60 mL)
For Serving
  • 2 limes
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 1 bean sprout
  • 1 bunch fresh thai basil
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 1 siracha
  • hoisin sauce
