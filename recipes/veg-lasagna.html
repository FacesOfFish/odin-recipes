Best Vegetable Lasagna

This is the Finest Vegetable Lasagna I Have EVER Tried!

One of my best friends is a vegetarian. She is also italian. If there's one thing that anyone knows, its that no one, NO ONE, does italian food better than an italian's mother. But we can't always get home easily, so I thought I would try to find a recipe that could make her feel like a little bit of her home was brought to her here.

Now she might be a vegetarian, but I don't have time for these recipes that fill up their lasagna with strange fillers like corn husk, and rice dust, and the gently grated leftover nibblings of a field mouse feasting on a tablecloth at 2am...

...so without further ado, I bring you: THE FINEST VEGETABLE LASAGNA RECIPE!

Ingredients

Veggies and Spinach
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 large carrots, chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium zucchini, chopped
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 5 to 6 ounces baby spinach
Tomato Sauce
  • 1 large can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • ¼ cup roughly chopped fresh basil + additional for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
Remaining Ingredients
  • 2 cups (16 ounces) low-fat cottage cheese, divided
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 9 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 8 ounces (2 cups) freshly grated low-moisture, part-skim mozzarella cheese
